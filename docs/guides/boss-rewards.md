## How the rewards work

- **Solo Leveling XP:** every boss pays **5 XP per point of its health**. A 10,000 HP boss = 50,000 XP; the 150,000 HP Ender Dragon = **750,000**. This is on top of the 3x XP gamerule.
- **Item bounty** (at the corpse, on top of normal loot), scaled by health and gated by tier:
  - **1,000 HP+** — diamonds (5 per 1k, up), emeralds, amethyst, XP bottles
  - **5,000 HP+** — + ancient debris, netherite scrap
  - **8,000 HP+** — + echo shards
  - **10,000 HP+** — + enchanted golden apples
- **First kill** of each boss type (per player): **double** XP.
- **Deathless fight** (no party death nearby): **+50%** XP and bounty.
- **50,000 HP+ bosses:** server-wide announcement + fireworks, and pay raw **SkillPoints** too.

Damage is a **multiplier** on everything the boss deals (players also take half damage from all mobs, so the effective hit is half the number shown).
